205 Mask 65.00 EUR Natural

Description:
An activating natural bio cellulose sheet mask soaked in 24 ml serum with several beneficial root extracts.

Key ingredients:
Algae extract, Sugars (Xylitols), Hyaluronic acid, Silica.

About the product

A hydrating natural 100% biodegradable bio cellulose sheet mask soaked in 24 ml serum. The sheet is made from ultra-fine fibres of cellulose produced by micro-organisms during the fermentation of coconut juice. Contains algae extract that is rich in minerals and detoxifies the skin. Different xylitols hydrates the skin and keeps it moist. Hyaluronic acid instantly hydrates the skin while silica helps retain the moisture all day. Effectively counteracts dehydration of really dry skin.

  • How to use

    1) Cleanse and wipe the face thoroughly 2) Open the sachet and unfold the mask 3) Pull off the two protective layers from the mask 4) Place the mask evenly on the face and leave on for 15-20 minutes 5) Gently pull off the mask and massage face with any excess serum using fingertips

  • INCI list:

    Aqua, Hamamelis virginiana (Witch hazel) leaf Water, Glycerin, Xylitylglucoside, Betaine,Anhydroxylitol, Xylitol, Lactic acid, Sodium dehydroacetate, Sodium Hyaluronate, Sodium benzoate, Potassium sorbate, Fucus vesiculosus (Seaweed) extract, Silica, Sodium phytate, Biosaccharide gum-1, Salicylic Acid, Glyceryl caprylate, Sodium anisate, Sodium levulinate, Citric acid, Alcohol.

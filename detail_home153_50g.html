About the product

Scented candle made of wax from organic soy with a scent of tabac. The candle is filled by hand in a mouth blown tinted amber glass. The candle has a burn time of more than 15 hours.

  • How to use

    Light the candle for shorter periods of time to maximise the scent and make it more long lasting. Cut the wick to a length of max 5 mm before lighting and relighting the candle. Make sure not to have candle lit for more than 2 hours at a time.

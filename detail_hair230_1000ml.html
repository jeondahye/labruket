230 Shampoo 72.00 EUR Birch

Description:
A natural shampoo that will cleanse and hydrate normal to oily hair and scalp.

Key ingredients:
Aloe vera juice, Birch extract.

About the product

A natural shampoo for normal to oily hair with extract of nettle that provides purifying properties and counteracts oiliness by reducing sebum production. Contains wheat protein that adds volume to the hair as well as aloe vera and oat to treat the hair and scalp. Organic and/or natural ingredients.

  • How to use

    Apply the shampoo to wet hair and massage carefully. Repeat if necessary. Rinse off with water. Keep in mind that it takes time (about 2 weeks) for the hair to adapt to a natural shampoo if you previously used a shampoo with parabenes and silicones.

  • INCI list:

    Aqua (Water), Coco-glucoside, Sodium coco sulfate, Aloe barbadensis (Aloe vera) leaf juice, Sodium chloride, Hydrolyzed wheat protein, Parfum(mix of essential oils), Glyceryl oleate, Glycerin, Citric acid, Citrus paradisi(Grapefruit) peel oil, Sodium benzoate, Potassium sorbate, Betula alba (Birch) bark extract, Mentha spicata (Spearmint) leaf oil, Maltodextrin, Citricacid, Beta glucan, Levulinic acid, Sodium levulinate and Limonene, Linalool (natural ingredients of essential oils).

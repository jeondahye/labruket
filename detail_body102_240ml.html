102 Hand cream 47.00 EUR Bergamot / Patchouli

Description:
A natural hand cream that moisturises and repairs dry and chapped hands.

Key ingredients:
Bergamot essential oil, Coconut oil, Meadowfoam oil, Patchouli essential oil, Shea butter.

About the product

Moisturising hand cream that hydrates and acts repairing on dry and chapped hands. Contains shea butter, coconut oil and meadowfoam that protects against free radicals and therefore counteracts both signs of ageing and age spots.Effectively tackles irritation of the skin and dry spots. Organic and/or natural ingredients.

  • How to use

    Use preferably after washing hands with L:a Bruket solid or liquid soaps or when needed. Can be used daily. Massage a small amount at the back of the hands and thereafter massage hands and cuticles with the cream.

  • INCI list:

    Aqua (Water), Glycerin, Sorbitan olivate, Betaine, Cetearyl olivate, Butyrospermum parkii (Shea) butter, Cocos nucifera (Coconut) oil, Limnanthes alba seed (Meadowfoam) oil, Cetyl palmitate, Cetyl alcohol, Stearyl alcohol, Sorbitan palmitate, Parfum, Benzyl alcohol, Sclerotium gum, Juniperus virginiana (Cedarwood) oil, Allantoin, Pogostemon cablin (Pa tchouli) oil, Citrus aurantium bergamia (Bergamot) fruit oil, Limonene, Salicylic acid, Sorbic acid, Linalool, Sodium hydroxide, Citral.

  • Active scent

    Bergamot / Patchouli A warm, fresh, herbal and spicy scent.
